Make Evangelism Personal

Our Workshops

Do you know of someone who does not know Jesus Christ, or is in desperate need to know him? Maybe you have tried to share your faith and share the Gospel of our Saviour, but failed at the attempt due to inadequate knowledge, lack of experience and fear of rejection that held you back from completely reaching out.

We as Evangelic Leaders must reach out to these people who have names, stories, hopes and fears, and pray and engage with them to spread the Gospel without fear.

Through partaking the journey in our Evangelism Training, you will learn to Focus on the Fundamentals and Practice these Fundamental skills in practical, real-life situations.

Evangelism is a Process not an event

What are the Fundamentals?

The Fundamentals that you will learn in our training course will include lessons on how to build relationships, how to pray effectively, how to present the Gospel, how to invite people to Faith, Church Planting and Discipleship Training.

Upon these fundamentals, you will also be required to practice, and encouraged during the duration of this course to practice these skills on the daily, to build both your skills and your confidence.

Our course is greatly based on Bible-based training, to equip and mobilize anyone to preach the Gospel of Jesus Christ.
