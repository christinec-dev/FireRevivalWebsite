THE 4 PILLARS OF OUR WOMENS MINISTRY

Pillars Of Support
Pillar One: Study

We must provide women with the opportunity to study God's word in a comfortable, safe and motivational environment.

2 Timothy 3:16-17(ESV), “All Scripture is breathed out by God and profitable for teaching, for reproof, for correction, and for training in righteousness, that the man of God may be competent, equipped for every good work.”

Pillar Two: Service

Women need opportunities to serve others. Doing so together, as the body of Christ, builds unity, encourages growth, and aids in developing relationships.

1 Peter 4:10 (ESV), “As each has received a gift, use it to serve one another, as good stewards of God’s varied grace.”

Pillar Three: Support

Women need safe places where they can find biblically-sound support. This could come in the form of prayer and guidance in different situations such as divorce, domestic violence, grief and any form of abuse. We also want these women to be protected and feel safe.

Ecclesiastes 4:10 (ESV), “For if they fall, one will lift up his fellow. But woe to him who is alone when he falls and has not another to lift him up!”

Pillar Four: Social

Women benefit by regularly meeting together with other believers and Ministry members. This offers them with opportunities for forming Christian friendships, and mature.

Hebrews 10:25 (ESV), “Not neglecting to meet together, as is the habit of some, but encouraging one another, and all the more as you see the Day drawing near.”

God Welcomes All

Who is the Womens Ministry For?

Our Womens Ministry here at Fire Revival Movement is open to all abused, divorced, broken, raped, widowed and desperate women who seek the comfort of God, and who needs a safe place to learn and recover through Jesus the Healer and Restorer.

As we minister the gift of God and all of his Power to them, we would like to give them a gift of love and support.

We are greatly asking the support of good-hearted individuals of the community and members of the Ministry to make donations so that we can give these ladies goodie-bags of food, clothes, toiletries and love, to help them on their spiritual journey of restoration and growth.
