What is The Movement?

Our Prophetic Assignment

God has given us a Prophetic Mantle Assignment, which is based on a promise given from Heaven.

ACTS 17:26 – “From one man he made all the nations, that they should inhabit the whole earth; and he marked out their appointed times in history and the boundaries of their lands.”

The Assignment based on ACTS says that we must go and display the power of the Holy Spirit publicly, so that those who witness it will come to salvation. Our Movement is a collection of Fire-Filled, Holy-Spirit filled nondenominational Evangelists/Revivalists, displaying His power all over South Africa, and soon in other parts of the African Continent.

About Our Movement

Fire Revival Movement was born after Beulah Vermeulen, a previous Jehovahs Witness, ministered in Kwazulu-Natal at a youth conference. On the way back, she and other ministry members experienced the supernaturnal movement of God, and saw people radically turning to God. Beulah asked the Lord, why did this happen, and that's when He opened the door for them by giving them a name for the ministry.

Serving under the covering of Pastors Frikkie & Bertah Spies from El-Shammah Ministries International, in Primrose Germiston, Beulah has received the assignment to change the nations of South Africa and take back our country by force and stir up revival in meetings, on the streets and in stadiums in all regions - witnessing the power of the Holy Spirit through signs and wonders.

Our goal at Fire Revival Movement is to make a difference in the lives of the less fortunate, spread His Gospel and change our country for the greater good.
